What does the PEIRTA offer you?

  • A triannual newsletter mailed to subscribers and available online.
  • Representation on pension and group insurance committees.
  • Discounts on curling (annual bonspiel) and golf. Other discounts may be negotiated.
  • Advocacy at the provincial and national levels (through membership in ACER-CART—the Canadian Association of Retired Teachers).
  • Annual General Meeting with guest speaker and free lunch.
  • Members only Facebook page with current information of interest to retired teachers.
