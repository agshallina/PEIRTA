About Us


The objectives of the Prince Edward Island Retired Teachers’ Association are to:

  • Maintain and increase professional and social contacts.
  • keep informed of developments in education. 
  • Meet with Government and negotiate better terms for retired teachers. 
  • Meet with outside organizations/merchants to obtain social/financial benefits for retired  teachers. 
  • Maintain a liaison with the PEI Teachers' Federation. 
  • Furnish recommendations and advice the provincial executive and other committees of the PEITF on matters affecting the welfare of retired teachers.
