Membership Application process


If you are not currently a member and wish to become one, contact Membership Chairperson: Cynthia MacDonald: Email: Cynthia MacDonald, or phone 902-566-4680.

Annual dues are $24.00/year

Dues for current members will be automatically deducted from your pension cheque.

It is the responsibility of each RTA member to notify the membership chair of any change in member's mailing address. Contact Cynthia MacDonald.

An application for membership form, with instructions, can be accessed at Membership Application Form. Print the form, fill it out, and then mail or email it to pensionsbanking@gov.pe.ca
